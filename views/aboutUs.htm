About us

It all started with us having our final spats with our managers. Resultant, without having any backup plan, we both resigned from our jobs. To sink into the reality, we went to a nearby pub, and in a very cinematic fashion, on a bar table, over a couple of drinks, the idea of Nebeskie was conceived. Like a fascinating start-up story, while having next round of drinks, Nebeskie's roadmap was laid.

No, Wait!

In actuality, nothing of this happened.

Nebeskie Labs Private Limited, the parent company of Earth Home, is the outcome of rigorous smart work, for more than two years, on the idea of bringing ease to our lives by infusion of latest technology. Earth Home was conceived with a focus on IoT products, for smart homes and a goal to provide cutting-edge technology at an affordable price. The goal was also to make our existing ecosystem energy efficient by way of monitoring. The company was incorporated on December 09, 2016 and received the Start-up India Recognition on May 26, 2017. Apart from adding innovation to our products, we are also determined to provide products of industry standard, which are safe and convenient to use.
