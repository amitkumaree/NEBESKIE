Imagination on switch boards

Earth Home Krayons are push button switches which have been designed especially for modern/retro Indian homes. The switches effortlessly fits into your existing standard wall panel hence enhancing the overall appearance of not only the wall panel but the whole wall itself. Krayons has been designed to dedicatedly work with Earth Home WIFI module only.

Variety Is The Only Constant

Krayons comes in a varied spectrum of colors to suit individual taste. The switches come with ambient lighting option, which adds another dimension at night. Also, the with the ambient lighting, it makes you aware of the state of the device.
