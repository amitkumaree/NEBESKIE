Earth Home App has been developed, with extreme precision, to help the user not only operate his smart home, but also to help him closely monitor the energy consumption. Consequently, this helps in making the ecosystem more energy efficient, hence, increasing your savings.

Get electricity units consumed daily/weekly/fortnightly/monthly with a single click on your smartphone. Not only that, you will be able to check which of your appliances are energy efficient and which are not. The data formats are customizable as per the requirement of the user.

The app presents the consumption data in graphs/charts or in ways most comprehensive to the user. The data formats are customizable so that it can be easily understood by the user in most effective and precise manner.
