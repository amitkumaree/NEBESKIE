Our Story

It all started with us having our final spats with our bosses at our respective offices. Resultant, we both resigned from our jobs, without having any backup plan. In order to sink in the reality, we went to a happening pub and in a very cenamatic fashion, the idea of Nebeskie was concieved on a bar table over a couple of drinks. Like a fascinating startup story, our road map was also decided over another round of drinks.

Wait...

Actually nothing of this happened.

Nebeskie Labs Private Limited aka Nebeskie, the parent company of Earth Home is the outcome of constant regerous smart work for more than two years on the idea to add ease to our lives by infusing latest technology. Nebeskie Was concieved with a focus on IoT products for smart home and a goal is to provide cutting edge technology at affordable price to make the existing ecosystem energy efficient. Company was incorporated on December, 09 2016 and received the Startup India Recognition on 26th May 2017. Along with innovation in our product we are determined to provide products of industry standard, which is safe and convenient for the user to use.
