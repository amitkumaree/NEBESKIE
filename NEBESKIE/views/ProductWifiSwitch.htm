Control your lights and other appliance from anywhere

Have control over your lights and appliances from anywhere using Earth Home Wi-Fi Switchs. These smart switchs retrofit into your existing wiring system and use local Wi-Fi network for seamless wireless control of your lights and appliances. Earth Home Wi-Fi switchs can operate any device which a traditional switch can, moreover, with a little technology infusion, you will be able to control it from anywhere ( for e.g. Turn on your air conditioner even before you reach home, turn off the lights of your house from anywhere, in case you forgot to do so). Use Earth Home smart switches with Earth Home crayons to make your wall come alive.

Schedule your lights and appliances

Schedule your lights for a worry free day. Turn off the light lamp at sunrise or synchronize your hall light with sunset. Schedule geyser for winter morning shower.

Safe Home is a happy home

Create an illusion of being at home when actually your are not. In one click, randomize your lights to make it appear like you are home. With a safe and protected home, now you can enjoy your vacations.
