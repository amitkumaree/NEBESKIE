earth home wifi switch

Control your lights and other appliance from anywhere

Control your lights and other appliances from anywhere using Earth Home Wi-Fi Switch .

This smart switch retrofit into your existing wiring system and uses local Wi-Fi network for wireless control of your lights and appliances.

Earth Home Wi-Fi switch can connect any device which a traditional switch does but with an innovation now you can control it from anywhere (few examples of the use cases) Use this smart switch with Earth Home crayons to make your wall come alive.

Schedule your lights

Schedule your lights for a worry free day.

Turn off the light lamp at sunrise or synchronize your hall light with sunset.

Safe Home is a happy home

Randomize your lights to make it appear like you are home – when you are not and have a safe and protected home.

All your connected lights can be randomized in one click.

System Requirement

  • Wi-Fi router
  • Android 4.4 or higher
  • Neutral wire required
  • Replaces single pole switch (Not compatible with two way switch
  • Works with your existing wiring system no additional wiring required.
