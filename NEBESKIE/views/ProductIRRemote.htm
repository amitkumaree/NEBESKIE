IR-Emote is another smart device which comes under the Earth Home umbrella of products and services. The state-of-the-art technology seamlessly replaces all the remotes at home with your smart phone.

It effortlessly replicates all the controls of all of your Infra Red remotes and enables your smart phone to do the same job.

Gone are the days of safely keeping one remote for TV, one for AC, one for music system, and many more. With Earth Home app, control TV, AC, music system is a hassle free manner.

With IR-Emote enabled homes, you can create ambience of your choice and taste. Pre-set the temperature of your room to ensure your comfort when you arrive home,. Set the controls to suit your requirements smartly with ultra ease.
